Main - Bt Builder vs. BTCS - Mailing List

To subscribe to the Bt Builder mailing list send email to btbuilder-request@identicalsoftware.com with a message body of "subscribe" (without the quotes).

The list restrict posts to subscribers only. To send a message to the list email btbuilder@identicalsoftware.com.

To unsubscribe send a message similar to the subscribe request except make the body of the massage "unsubscribe".
